Course Structure & Content

For Classes 11 and 12, we offer comprehensive coaching in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Mathematics and Physics are taught by Virendra Prasad Trivedi Sir, while Chemistry and Biology are handled by Vishal Verma Sir. Visit our Courses page for detailed syllabus information.

Our competitive exam coaching (NDA, CDS, AFCAT, CSAT) is designed with a focus on the specific exam pattern and syllabus. It includes in-depth subject-wise lectures, extensive practice problems, regular mock tests, performance analysis, and basic guidance for subsequent stages like SSB Interview (for defence exams). Specialized faculty covers all required sections including Mathematics, General Ability Test, English, General Knowledge, and Reasoning.

Faculty & Teaching Methodology

AV Tutorial is led by our highly experienced founders: Virendra Prasad Trivedi Sir (Mathematics & Physics, teaching since 1992) and Vishal Verma Sir (Chemistry & Biology, teaching since 2005). You can learn more about them on our Teachers page.

We emphasize strong conceptual clarity. Dedicated doubt-clearing sessions are an integral part of our program. Students are encouraged to ask questions during and after classes. We ensure all doubts are resolved promptly to prevent any accumulation of confusion.
