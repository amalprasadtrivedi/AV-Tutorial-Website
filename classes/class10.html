Subjects Covered in Class 10

Mathematics

Taught by Virendra Prasad Trivedi Sir. Covers Real Numbers, Polynomials, Pair of Linear Equations, Quadratic Equations, Arithmetic Progressions, Triangles, Coordinate Geometry, Introduction to Trigonometry & its Applications, Circles, Constructions, Areas Related to Circles, Surface Areas & Volumes, and Statistics & Probability. Emphasis on board exam patterns and tricky problems.

Physics

Taught by Virendra Prasad Trivedi Sir. Explores Light (Reflection & Refraction), Human Eye & Colourful World, Electricity, Magnetic Effects of Electric Current, and Sources of Energy. Focus on derivations, diagrams, and numerical applications as per board requirements.

Chemistry

Taught by Vishal Verma Sir. Covers Chemical Reactions & Equations, Acids, Bases & Salts, Metals & Non-metals, Carbon & Its Compounds, and Periodic Classification of Elements. Emphasis on balancing equations, reactions mechanisms, and properties.

Biology

Taught by Vishal Verma Sir. Includes Life Processes, Control & Coordination, How Do Organisms Reproduce?, Heredity & Evolution, and Our Environment. Emphasis on detailed diagrams, biological processes, and their significance.
