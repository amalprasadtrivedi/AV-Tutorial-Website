Class 11 Programs

In-depth preparation for Intermediate examinations and competitive entrance tests.

Our Class 11 coaching program at AV Tutorial is meticulously crafted to provide students with an in-depth understanding of core concepts in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. This year is pivotal for students aspiring for competitive examinations like JEE, NEET, NDA, and others, as it covers a significant portion of their syllabus. Subjects Covered in Class 11

Mathematics

Taught by Virendra Prasad Trivedi Sir. Covers Sets, Relations & Functions, Trigonometric Functions, Principle of Mathematical Induction, Complex Numbers & Quadratic Equations, Linear Inequalities, Permutations & Combinations, Binomial Theorem, Sequences & Series, Straight Lines, Conic Sections, Introduction to 3D Geometry, Limits & Derivatives, Mathematical Reasoning, and Statistics & Probability.

Physics

Taught by Virendra Prasad Trivedi Sir. Explores Physical World & Measurement, Kinematics, Laws of Motion, Work, Energy & Power, Motion of System of Particles & Rigid Body, Gravitation, Properties of Bulk Matter, Thermodynamics, Behavior of Perfect Gases & Kinetic Theory, Oscillations, and Waves. Strong emphasis on derivations and numerical problem-solving.

Chemistry

Taught by Vishal Verma Sir. Covers Some Basic Concepts of Chemistry, Structure of Atom, Classification of Elements & Periodicity in Properties, Chemical Bonding & Molecular Structure, States of Matter, Thermodynamics, Equilibrium, Redox Reactions, Hydrogen, s-Block Elements, p-Block Elements (Group 13 & 14), Organic Chemistry (Some Basic Principles & Techniques), Hydrocarbons, and Environmental Chemistry.

Biology

Taught by Vishal Verma Sir. Includes The Living World, Biological Classification, Plant Kingdom, Animal Kingdom, Morphology of Flowering Plants, Anatomy of Flowering Plants, Structural Organisation in Animals, Cell: The Unit of Life, Biomolecules, Cell Cycle & Cell Division, Transport in Plants, Mineral Nutrition, Photosynthesis in Higher Plants, Respiration in Plants, Plant Growth & Development, Digestion & Absorption, Breathing & Exchange of Gases, Body Fluids & Circulation, Excretory Products & their Elimination, Locomotion & Movement, Neural Control & Coordination, and Chemical Coordination & Integration.
