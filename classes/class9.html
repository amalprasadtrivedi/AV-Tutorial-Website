Subjects Covered in Class 9

Mathematics

Taught by Virendra Prasad Trivedi Sir. Covers all topics including Number Systems, Algebra (Polynomials, Linear Equations), Geometry (Euclid's Geometry, Lines & Angles, Triangles, Quadrilaterals, Circles), Coordinate Geometry, Mensuration, and Statistics & Probability. Focus on problem-solving techniques.

Physics

Taught by Virendra Prasad Trivedi Sir. Explores fundamental concepts of Motion, Force and Laws of Motion, Gravitation, Work and Energy, Sound, and Light (basic principles). Emphasis on practical applications and numerical problems.

Chemistry

Taught by Vishal Verma Sir. Covers Matter in Our Surroundings, Is Matter Around Us Pure, Atoms and Molecules, and Structure of the Atom. Focus on chemical reactions and basic atomic theory.

Biology

Taught by Vishal Verma Sir. Includes The Fundamental Unit of Life (Cell), Tissues, Diversity in Living Organisms, Why Do We Fall Ill, and Natural Resources. Emphasis on diagrams and biological processes.
