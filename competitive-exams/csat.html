Excel in Civil Services Aptitude Test (CSAT)

The Civil Services Aptitude Test (CSAT) is a crucial component of the UPSC Civil Services Preliminary Examination, designed to assess the analytical abilities, comprehension skills, and general aptitude of aspiring civil servants. At AV Tutorial, our CSAT coaching program provides focused and comprehensive preparation, ensuring you develop the necessary skills to clear this qualifying paper with confidence. Under the expert guidance of Virendra Prasad Trivedi Sir (Basic Numeracy & Logical Reasoning) and Vishal Verma Sir (Data Interpretation & General Mental Ability), we provide in-depth concept clarity, extensive practice, and strategic approaches to solve complex problems efficiently.

CSAT Exam Syllabus Covered

Comprehension

Focus on improving reading speed, understanding complex passages, inferring conclusions, and identifying the central theme.

Interpersonal Skills including Communication Skills

Covers various aspects of effective communication, non-verbal cues, active listening, and conflict resolution scenarios relevant to decision-making.

Logical Reasoning & Analytical Ability

Includes topics like puzzles, seating arrangements, blood relations, syllogism, coding-decoding, series, analogies, etc. Emphasis on developing logical thinking.

Decision Making & Problem Solving

Training on approaching ethical dilemmas and practical problems with a structured mindset to arrive at the most appropriate solutions.

General Mental Ability

Covers a range of questions designed to test overall mental alertness and quick thinking, including various types of verbal and non-verbal aptitude.

Basic Numeracy & Data Interpretation

Taught by Virendra Prasad Trivedi Sir. Covers Numbers and their relations, Orders of magnitude, Percentages, Ratio & Proportion, Averages, Profit & Loss, Time & Work, Time & Distance. Data Interpretation includes Charts, Graphs, Tables, and Data Sufficiency. (Class X level).
