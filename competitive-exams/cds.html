CDS Entrance Exam Preparation

Your path to becoming an officer in the Indian Armed Forces.

Achieve Your Defence Aspirations with CDS Coaching

The Combined Defence Services (CDS) examination, conducted by UPSC, is a crucial step for graduates aspiring to join the Indian Military Academy (IMA), Indian Naval Academy (INA), Air Force Academy (AFA), and Officers' Training Academy (OTA). At AV Tutorial, our CDS coaching program provides focused and comprehensive preparation for the Written Examination, covering English, General Knowledge, and Elementary Mathematics (for IMA/INA/AFA). With expert guidance from Virendra Prasad Trivedi Sir (Mathematics) and Vishal Verma Sir (Science & General Knowledge), combined with strategic mentorship for the SSB Interview, we equip you with the knowledge and confidence to succeed.
