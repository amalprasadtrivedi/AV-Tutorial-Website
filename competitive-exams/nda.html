NDA Exam Syllabus Covered

Paper I: Mathematics

Taught by Virendra Prasad Trivedi Sir. Covers Algebra, Matrices and Determinants, Trigonometry, Analytical Geometry of Two and Three Dimensions, Differential Calculus, Integral Calculus and Differential Equations, Vector Algebra, and Statistics and Probability. Focus on exam-oriented problem-solving and time management techniques.

Paper II: General Ability Test - Part A (English)

Covers Grammar and Usage, Vocabulary, Comprehension, and Cohesion. Focus on improving communication skills, error detection, sentence improvement, and precise writing.

Paper II: General Ability Test - Part B (Science)

Physics (by Virendra Prasad Trivedi Sir): Properties of Matter, Mechanics, Heat, Light, Electricity, Magnetism. Chemistry (by Vishal Verma Sir): Physical & Chemical changes, Elements, Mixtures & Compounds, Acids, Bases, Salts, Carbon, Water, Fertilizers. Biology (by Vishal Verma Sir): Living & Non-living, Basics of Life (Cell, Tissues), Human Systems, Food, Diseases.

Paper II: General Ability Test - Part B (General Knowledge)

Covers History, Geography, Current Events, and Indian Polity & Economy. Focus on significant historical events, geographical features of India & the world, recent national & international developments, and basic civics.
