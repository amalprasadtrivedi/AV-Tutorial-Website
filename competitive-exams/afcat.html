Achieve Your Air Force Dream with AFCAT Coaching

The Air Force Common Admission Test (AFCAT) is the gateway for graduates to join the Flying and Ground Duty (Technical and Non-Technical) branches of the Indian Air Force. At AV Tutorial, our AFCAT coaching program offers comprehensive preparation for the written examination, covering General Awareness, Verbal Ability, Numerical Ability, and Reasoning & Military Aptitude. Our expert faculty, including Virendra Prasad Trivedi Sir (Numerical Ability) and Vishal Verma Sir (General Awareness - Science portion), provides in-depth conceptual clarity, rigorous practice, and strategic guidance to help you navigate this competitive exam and clear your path to a distinguished career in the IAF.

AFCAT Exam Syllabus Covered

General Awareness

Covers History, Geography, Civics, Indian Polity, Economy, Basic Science (by Vishal Verma Sir for Chemistry & Biology, Trivedi Sir for Physics), Environmental Studies, Current Affairs, and Defence related topics.

Verbal Ability in English

Focuses on Comprehension, Error Detection, Sentence Completion, Synonyms & Antonyms, Testing of Vocabulary, Idioms and Phrases. Aims to enhance overall English language proficiency.

Numerical Ability

Taught by Virendra Prasad Trivedi Sir. Covers Decimal & Fractions, Time & Work, Average, Profit & Loss, Percentage, Ratio & Proportion, Simple & Compound Interest, Speed, Distance & Time. Emphasis on quick problem-solving techniques.

Reasoning & Military Aptitude Test

Includes Verbal Reasoning, Non-Verbal Reasoning, Data Interpretation, and various Military Aptitude tests. Designed to assess mental ability and suitability for the armed forces.

Note: Engineering Knowledge Test (EKT) coaching for Technical Branch aspirants can also be provided on request.
