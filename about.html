About AV Tutorial

Your journey to academic excellence starts here.

Our Story & Mission

AV Tutorial was founded in 1992 by Virendra Prasad Trivedi Sir, a visionary educator with a profound passion for Mathematics and Physics. What began as a humble coaching endeavor focused on imparting deep conceptual understanding, has blossomed into a comprehensive learning institution. Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, critical thinking skills, and the confidence to not only excel in their academic pursuits but also to achieve their aspirations in competitive examinations.

In 2005, the team was further strengthened by the arrival of Vishal Verma Sir, an equally dedicated and talented teacher specializing in Chemistry and Biology. His expertise completed our vision, allowing AV Tutorial to offer holistic science education, ensuring students receive top-tier guidance across all core subjects.

We are committed to creating a supportive and stimulating learning environment where every student feels encouraged to ask questions, explore ideas, and reach their full potential.

Our Philosophy of Education

Conceptual Clarity

We emphasize building strong foundational concepts rather than rote learning, ensuring students truly understand the 'why' behind every solution.

Personalized Attention

Our small batch sizes and dedicated faculty allow for individualized guidance, catering to each student's unique learning pace and needs.

Experienced Mentorship

With decades of combined teaching experience, Trivedi Sir and Verma Sir bring invaluable insights, effective teaching methodologies, and a passion for mentoring.

Problem-Solving Focus

We equip students with a robust toolkit of problem-solving strategies, preparing them to tackle diverse challenges in both school and competitive exams.

Holistic Development

Beyond academics, we aim to foster critical thinking, confidence, and a lifelong love for learning, shaping well-rounded individuals.

Results-Oriented Approach

Our structured curriculum, regular assessments, and continuous feedback mechanisms are designed to maximize student performance and ensure tangible results.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in teaching and student achievement.
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and ethical practices in all our interactions.
  • Dedication: Committed to the success and growth of every student.
  • Innovation: Continuously evolving our teaching methods to stay effective and relevant.
  • Support: Providing a nurturing environment where students feel supported and confident.
