Virendra Prasad Trivedi Sir

Mathematics & Physics

Teaching since 1992 (33+ Years Experience)

M.Sc. Mathematics, B.Ed.

Virendra Prasad Trivedi Sir, the esteemed founder of AV Tutorial, is a highly respected educator with over three decades of teaching experience in Mathematics and Physics. His profound understanding of the subjects, coupled with an innate ability to simplify complex concepts, has made him a favorite among students. Trivedi Sir's teaching philosophy centers on building a strong conceptual foundation, encouraging critical thinking, and fostering a deep love for learning.

He has successfully guided countless students through their Class 9-12 board examinations, helping them achieve top scores. Beyond school academics, his specialized coaching for competitive exams like NDA, CDS, AFCAT, and CSAT has enabled many aspirants to fulfill their dreams of joining prestigious forces and civil services. His dedication to each student's success is unwavering, and he believes in a personalized approach to help every individual reach their maximum potential.

Trivedi Sir is also the driving force behind the "Kalindri Gyan" YouTube channel, where he shares his knowledge with a wider audience, demonstrating his commitment to accessible and quality education. His mentorship extends beyond the classroom, shaping not just academicians but confident and well-rounded individuals.

Vishal Verma Sir

Chemistry & Biology

Teaching since 2005 (20+ Years Experience)

M.Sc. Chemistry

Vishal Verma Sir brings over two decades of dynamic teaching experience in Chemistry and Biology to AV Tutorial. Known for his engaging teaching style and ability to make even the most challenging scientific principles relatable, Verma Sir has played a pivotal role in expanding AV Tutorial's comprehensive science offerings. His passion for the biological and chemical sciences is infectious, inspiring students to explore these fields with enthusiasm.

Verma Sir excels at breaking down complex reactions and biological processes into easily digestible information, using real-world examples and practical applications. He focuses on developing strong problem-solving skills and encourages students to think scientifically. Under his guidance, students not only achieve excellent results in their school examinations but also build a solid foundation for future studies in science and medicine.
